TIPS TO PREPARE THE TAMAL

I suggest making the marinade a couple of days before to make the process easier, then the next day marinade the meat and place it in fridge overnight for extra flavor. Then the next day assemble the tamales and you can cook them the same day or keep them wrapped in the fridge and cook them the day after.
