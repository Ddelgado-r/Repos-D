COLOMBIA

Colombia or politically known as the Democratic Republic of Colombia is a country located at south of the American continent and it has boundaries with countries like Panama, Venezuela, Ecuador, Peru and Brazil and also is surrounded by the Pacific and Caribbean ocean. Colombia is full of a cultural biodiversity as well as an incredible wildlife and vegetation.

As I said before Colombia has different cultures where each one has its traditions and dialects. Being said that, We are going to see some of the most tradional dishes at Colombia and how to prepare them step by step.


Colombian Recipies

To select five dishes from Colombia is a hard work and that's cause we have an inmensity of dishes to choose from, but in this case I have brought to you the dishes that in my opinion are the most iconic and delicious:

  1. Bandeja Paisa: This platter is probably the most popular Colombian dish, originally from the Andean region of the country where the people are called “Paisas” and the area where I was born and raised.
  2. Tamales: There are many variations of tamales in Colombia, but they all have something in common and that is that Colombian Tamales are all wrapped in banana leaves.
  3. Sudado de Pollo: This chicken stew is one of the most popular dishes in Colombian homes. It's delicious served with white rice on the side which soaks up the flavorful sauce.
  4. Pescado Frito: This Colombian-Style Fried Whole Fish is a very popular dish from the coast of Colombia. Some of the most popular fish in Colombia used to make this dish are: red snapper, mojarra and tilapia.
  5. Chuleta Valluna: This pork milanese is one of the most popular dishes in Colombian Restaurants. This Chuleta Valluna is a traditional dish from El Valle department of Colombia.
