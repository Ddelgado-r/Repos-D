BANDEJA PAISA(PAISA PLATTER)


Bandeja Paisa is a traditional Colombian dish from the Andean region of the country. It is a filling and delicious recipe that contains beans, rice, arepa, chorizo, plantain, chicharr√≥n, avocado and beef.


HOW TO PREPARE THE BANDEJA PAISA

  1. Prepare the beans, hogao and powdered beef one day ahead and keep them in the refrigerator.
  2. When you are going to serve the bandeja paisa, heat the beans and powdered beef and hogao. Make or fry the chicharrones.
  3. Cook the white rice and plantains.
  4. Fry the eggs and chorizos.
  5. To serve, place the rice in a tray or platter and place the rest of the ingredients as you like. I prefer the beans in a separate bowl, but you can ladle the beans next to the rice if you like.
  6. Bring the hogao in a serving dish to the table, so people can place it on top of the beans if they like.
